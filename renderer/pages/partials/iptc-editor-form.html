Use placeholders like {name}, {number}, {team}, {car_model}, {nationality}, {category} in Caption Template, Base Keywords, and Person Shown — they will be replaced with each participant's actual data from the table below.

Multi-pilot photos (WEC, IMSA, endurance, etc.): wrap the per-pilot section in [[ ]] to repeat it for each matched pilot. Example: Race 2026 [[#{number} {name}]] becomes Race 2026 #1 Driver A, #2 Driver B when two pilots are detected in the same frame.
